PANDORA: The Open-Source, Structurally Elastic Humanoid Robot

Humanoids 2024

ArXiv PANDORA uses linear series-elastic actuators placed in series and parallel configurations, with pairs of actuators controlled by a single motor controller. I designed the high-level controls code to allow control of the robot at the motor controller level (useful for testing), the actuator level (also useful for testing), and the pin joint level (used for whole body control). I also contributed other small bits, such as test routines for task-space control and a driver for our IMU.
